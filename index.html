About Us

Years of Experience
Introducing Smart Farming

Agrigain Locura Ventures, a pioneering Nigerian agritech startup, was registered in February 2022. Our core mission is to revolutionize the agrocommodity supply chain by providing streamlined sourcing and supply services, coupled with innovative agricultural practices. Operating primarily in Kaduna, Bauchi, Kebbi, and Borno, we have established a robust network of 216 small scale farmers cultivating rice, beans, soybeans, sesame seeds, and hibiscus.

Why We Started

Our Story

We started Agrigain from the thought of having a safe means of supplying agricultural products to people with limited sourcing scope. Now, we are focused on not only serving the best products but also working to change the economy of Nigeria's agriculture sector and that of Africa at large by creating direct market linkages for small-medium scale farmers and adding value to general agro products. Through our comprehensive approach, we empower small-scale rice farmers with quality inputs, process their produce into value-added products, facilitate secure market linkages, optimize logistics, and streamline warehousing. By making the commodity value chain flow more efficiently, we contribute to the growth and sustainability of agriculture in Nigeria.
