About Us

Years of Experience
Introducing Smart Farming

Agrigain Locura Ventures is a dynamic Nigerian agri-tech startup revolutionizing agriculture. Founded in 2022, our mission is to empower small-scale rice farmers through holistic agribusiness solutions, bridging the gap between producers and consumers for a sustainable future.

Our Services

Business Identity

We blend innovation with tradition, focusing on sustainable impact and crop yield improvement. Our forward-thinking approach and commitment to quality drive Agrigain Locura Ventures.

Our business identity is rooted in a forward-thinking approach that combines technological advancements with traditional agricultural practices. We are committed to making a lasting impact on crop yield improvement and increasing the average income of our partner farmers across the global market.

Sustainability Plan

Our business model is designed for the long term by integrating technology, training, and quality assurance into our operations. The solar-powered irrigation project is a testament to our commitment to environmentally friendly and economically viable agricultural practices.

Business Aims
  • Increased Crop Yield: Leverage technology and training to enhance crop yields, ensuring food security and economic prosperity.
  • Improved Crop Quality: Dedicated product line and stringent quality control to build trust among consumers.
  • Increasing Farmers' Income: Empower small-scale farmers with inputs, knowledge, and technology to elevate their average income.

Agrigain Locura Ventures is not just a business; it is a catalyst for positive change in Nigeria's agricultural landscape.
