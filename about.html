Introducing Smart Farming

Agrigain Locura Ventures, is a dynamic Nigerian agri-tech startup that's revolutionizing the agricultural landscape. Founded in 2022, our mission is to empower and uplift small-scale rice farmers across Nigeria through a holistic approach to agribusiness. Agrigain Ventures is at the forefront of innovation, reshaping the way Nigeria interacts with and benefits from its rich agricultural resources. Our mission is to bridge the gap between producers and consumers, ensuring that agro-commodities are accessible, affordable, and sustainable for all. At Agrigain Locura Ventures, our mission is crystal clear – to revolutionize the agricultural value chain and uplift the livelihoods of small-scale rice farmers. We believe that agriculture is the backbone of Nigeria's economy, and by providing holistic solutions, we aim to boost farm productivity, increase income, and contribute to the nation's agricultural growth.
